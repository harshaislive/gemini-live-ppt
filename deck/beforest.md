# Beforest

## Brand definition

Beforest is a **nature-first lifestyle collective**.
It is presented first as a **land-restoration story**, then as a membership model.

Core idea:
- restore land
- create landscapes where people belong
- help people protect part of the year for reset, rhythm, and return

## Brand belief

The central Beforest belief is:

> Protect 10% of the year, and the other 90% gets better.

This is framed as:
- protection, not subtraction
- rhythm, not indulgence
- recurring reset, not one-off escape
- calibration, not consumption

## Audience

Beforest is aimed at:
- urban professionals
- successful but exhausted people
- people normalized to running at capacity
- people who do not need another productivity tool
- people who need permission and structure to protect recovery

## Voice and tone

Beforest should sound:
- quiet and certain
- disciplined
- honest
- premium without elitism
- grounded, direct, and calm

It should feel like:
- one thoughtful person speaking to another
- someone who does not oversell
- someone who knows what matters

## What Beforest emphasizes

- rhythm
- practice
- belonging
- calibration
- reset
- intentional living
- restoration of land
- transformation through return

## What Beforest is not

Do **not** frame Beforest as:
- a vacation
- a holiday
- a getaway
- an escape
- a deal
- a budget offer
- a value buy
- real estate
- a property investment
- a conventional resort membership
- a timeshare in the usual sense

## Hard brand rules

Never:
- break price into per-night or per-day math
- cheapen the story with hospitality pricing language
- invent facts, numbers, locations, or promises
- sound like a hard-sell presentation
- speak to a crowd instead of one person
- frame the trial stay as a fallback option

Always:
- speak with conviction
- stay grounded in approved project facts
- treat each slide like a scene
- treat the trial stay as the **pilot / first 1%**
- let the land carry proof where possible

## Emotional arc

Beforest presentations are designed to move through:
- tension
- reframe
- proof
- experience
- clarity
- urgency
- action

This means the story should:
1. name the exhaustion
2. offer the reframe
3. earn trust with evidence
4. make the life feel real
5. explain the structure clearly
6. show the cost of delay
7. invite the first real step

## Proof points

Approved proof points repeatedly used across materials:
- 7 years of work
- 6 collectives
- 1,300 acres restored from degraded farmland
- 250 families already inside the rhythm

## Design intent

Beforest deck design is meant to be:
- image-first
- immersive
- minimal in visible text
- emotionally paced
- mobile-friendly

Core design rules from source materials:
- heading below the main image area, not on top of it
- supporting text capped tightly
- subtitles at the bottom of the image stage
- one dominant image surface on mobile
- simple wrap-up and CTA state

## CTA philosophy

Primary action:
- book a stay at Blyton Bungalow, Coorg

Why:
- the land explains Beforest better than slides do
- people should feel it before making the bigger decision

Closing line used in the approved materials:
- `You decide with your feet, not your eyes. See you in the slow lane.`

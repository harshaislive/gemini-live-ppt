# Beforest 10% Lifestyle product knowledge

## What it is
The 10% Lifestyle membership by Beforest gives access, not ownership. It is framed as 30 person-nights per year for 10 years across Beforest collectives. It is a recurring reset practice, not a leisure plan.

## Key numbers
- 17.6 lakhs introductory pricing for 10 years of access
- 30 person-nights per year across the decade
- 300 nights of intentional living across 10 years
- 6 collectives currently operating
- 1,300 acres restored from degraded farmland
- 250 families already members
- 7 years of proven impact

## Pricing framing
- Never break pricing into per-night or per-day math.
- Frame the structure as rhythm and practice, not cost efficiency.
- The acceptable framing is 30 nights per year for 10 years, or 300 nights of intentional living.
- This is a premium offer and should not be described as cheap, affordable, value-oriented, or budget-friendly.

## What it is not
- Not real estate
- Not a property investment
- Not a resort membership
- Not a timeshare in spirit, even if some transaction mechanics sound superficially similar
- Not carry-forward access; nights do not accumulate

## Structure details
- Immediate family can participate
- Children under 12 do not count toward person-nights
- Nights cannot be carried forward because the aim is rhythm, not accumulation
- A 2:1 weekday-to-weekend balance helps keep access workable for everyone
- Access is subject to availability

## Trial stay
- Book Blyton Bungalow in Coorg as the pilot experience
- The land explains Beforest more clearly than a presentation can
- The full amount spent on the trial stay adjusts toward membership when someone joins
- Visiting first reduces uncertainty and adds certainty
